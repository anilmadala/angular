 Meet RateFast

RateFast was created by a network of doctors with years of treating patients in the California Workers' Compensation system. We have a hands on treating background drawn from a wide diversity of specialties. We found a great variance of measurement reporting, documentation, accuracy and ratability in PR-4 reports. We knew there had to be a better way. Treating doctors need a simple, easy and fun system. We designed this system to meet those needs.

 Train

RateFast is designed to make every process of worker's compensation reports more simple and intuitive. Our focus is on getting an accurate, reproducible impairment rating, every time. So using RateFast is easy because it is software specifically designed to guide you through the correct steps for rating impairment. You can use RateFast on any mobile device, personal computer, and from most major browsers. We have a free help desk line and a videos series to help you get started.
