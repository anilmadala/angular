RateFast (PR2)

A PR2 report is an interval report required by law to be provided every six week in open and active workers' compensation claims. For the report to be considered accurate it must describe the indication for the revisit, the current status of the injured worker, a pertinent physical exam, and reasoned recommendations for medications, imaging, consultations and/or interventions (i.e. injections, surgery, etc.).

A well written PR2 report helps move the claim along in an efficient and logical process. A poorly organized report will result in delays of authorization, Utilization Review calls, and possibly prolonged disability.
