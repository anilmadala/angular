Doctor's First 5021

A Doctor's First Report (Form 5021) is first injury summary report that is required by the Division of California Workers' Compensation when a worker's compensation claim presents to the medical provider's office. This report is critical to the filing of the claim, as it must accurately describe the mechanism of injury, physical exam findings, diagnosis, and treatment plan. Furthermore, this report contains the provider's opinion on work relatedness "causation". It is here that the report has to outline in detail the duration of treatment and recovery time, and also clearly state any functional work restrictions for the injured worker to return to work.
