Every user has a "Role" in managing the practice account. Admin users can invite new users, change information about existing users, and update the settings for the Practice Account. Normal Non-Admin users can only change information related to their own personal account.

All users can create new work comp reports. The role of Admin only relates to managing users in the Practice Account.

A user's Report Access Level determines how much power a user has to edit and close a report. While all users can create reports and download closed reports, some sections of reports are restricted to certain users.


1 - Staff - Users with this level can only create reports and enter basic information about the injury and the patient. Users with this level are restricted from accessing areas of the report that involve medical details from the examination. Usually, people with this level are front-desk staff.

2 - Medical Assistant - Users with this level have access to more areas than level 1 users. They can input details about the patient's history and current condition, but they cannot access areas of the report related to medical decision making. This role could include staff, nurses, and other assistants.

3 - NP/PA - Users with this level have full access to all areas of the report. They can be assigned as the treating physician for a particular visit. This role is usually assigned to NPs and PAs who examine the patient and make medical decisions, but who do not actually submit and sign the report.

4 - Physician - Users with this level can submit and close reports. This role should be assigned to physicians, as well as NPs and PAs who are authorized to sign off on reports. When a user submits a report, his/her digital signature will appear in signature areas, as well as his/her credentials, license number, and other details.
